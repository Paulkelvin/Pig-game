How to play

  • Click "Roll dice" to garner points
  • The first player to garner up to 150points wins the game.
  • If a dice number one is rolled, all point(s) accumulated in the "current" panel is rendered back to 0 and a turn is forfeited
  • To save garnered points, click on the "hold" button, but a turn will be forfeited.
